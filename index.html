How to play

Touch/mouse

  • To pick consonants and vowels click their respective buttons
  • Tap the letter you want to use and it will be automatically moved to the second row. To remove a letter tap the second row

Keyboard

  • To pick consonants and vowels type 'c' and 'v' respectively
  • Type the letter you want to use and it will be automatically moved to the second row. To remove a letter type backspace or delete
